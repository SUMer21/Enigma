Enigma

Enigma Project: Unraveling the Mystery of Code and Cipher Inspired by the iconic Enigma machine showcased in The Imitation Game, this project brings the allure of encryption and decoding to a modern digital platform. The Enigma Project is designed to convert any simple string message into a complex, secret-coded string, making it unreadable without the right key. With just one pass through the system, messages transform into coded texts, and a second pass effortlessly decrypts them back to the original message. Our aim is to explore the art and science of cryptography, highlighting the brilliance behind historical encryption and its modern applications in data security.
